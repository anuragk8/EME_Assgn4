About Me

My name is Anurag Kelkar, and I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science at the University of Florida, with a strong academic record (GPA: 3.89/4). My coursework includes specialized subjects such as Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Distributed Operating System Principles. I have a robust technical foundation, with proficiency in programming languages like Python, Java, C/C++, and JavaScript, as well as experience in databases including MySQL, MongoDB, and PostgreSQL. My skills extend to using modern tools like TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Kubernetes, enabling me to tackle challenging projects effectively.

During my time at Securonix India, I gained hands-on experience in CI/CD and Microservices, contributing to large-scale software development and deployment. I automated tasks with Python, reducing resource management time by 80%, and optimized Kubernetes microservice deployments, improving efficiency by 40%. I also played a critical role in scaling and maintaining the SOAR platform, ensuring seamless upgrades in production environments. My exposure to Agile development has equipped me to collaborate efficiently in dynamic team settings, delivering solutions that meet real-world challenges.

My project portfolio demonstrates my passion for solving problems using cutting-edge technology. I developed a Progressive Rock Classifier with 89.67% accuracy using neural networks and optimized data processing with PCA to enhance model efficiency. For my undergraduate capstone project, I built a news aggregator leveraging machine learning, achieving 90% accuracy in clustering events and publishing a paper on my findings. I am driven by the pursuit of knowledge and innovation, eager to bring my expertise and problem-solving mindset to meaningful challenges in computer science.
